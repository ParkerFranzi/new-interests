How to use

Enter your favorite author, game, book, movie, podcast, or show. You can enter in multiple at the same time seperated by a comma(,). If one thing shares a title between different types you can also specify type by adding in the type followed by a colon before your search term ex. show:Game of Thrones

Once you've got your results, click on the name and you'll see some related news articles and YouTube videos to learn more about them!
